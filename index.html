My Journey into Tech

From Curiosity to Coding

Meet Andrea, a young woman who recently decided to embark
on a new and exciting journey into the world of technology
and coding. She had always been curious about how websites
and applications worked, but never imagined she would start
learning how to to build them herself.

The First steps

Andrea began by learning the basics of HTML, the foundation
of web development. She created her first webpage, adding
headings, paragraphs, and even formatting her text using
boldd, italic, and underline elements

Every new tag she learned brought a sense of
accomplishment.
She quickly became comfortable with HTML elements and is
eager to explore more.
Next on her list: learning more HTML elements and CSS to
style her web pages!

Challenges and Growth

The journey wasn't always easy. Andrea encountered errors,
bugs, and challenges that tested her patience. But she never
gave up. She knew that with every mistake, she was one step
closer to beecoming a confident coder.

At the end of this program, Andrea can build web pages and
will slowly venture into javaScript to make her projects
interactive.

The Road Ahead

Andrea knows that the road to mastering tech is long, but she is
excited for the future. She's even started sharing her progress
on social media to others, especially other women, to
dive into the world of coding.

Conclusion

Andrea's journey is just beginning. With passion,
determination, and the ability to never stop learning, she
knows that the possibilities in tech are endless.
